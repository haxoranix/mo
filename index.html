NEXT THING I KNEW…

…I realized I was already living the job I wanted to create.

Hi, I’m Mo Nariani.

Most evenings, you will find me in a theater, at a film festival, watching a documentary, attending a comedy show, or listening to live storytelling somewhere in Los Angeles.

Not because I have to. Because that is where I genuinely want to be.

My background is in sales. My education is in film. My favorite place is somewhere between the opening curtain and the closing credits.

Eventually, a question occurred to me. What would happen if those two worlds spent more time together?

That is why I created Next Thing I Knew.

Not simply to sell sponsorships, but to introduce remarkable people to one another. To help businesses become patrons of culture. To help artists spend less time chasing funding and more time creating unforgettable experiences.

I do not believe great partnerships are sold.

I believe they are discovered.
Curated.
And nurtured for years to come.

Mo Nariani Founder & Chief Partnership Curator
